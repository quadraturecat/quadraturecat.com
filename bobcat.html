Bobcat

A PC/104 Form Factor Controller and Radio

Overview

Bobcat is a low power microcontroller board with a range of control interfaces and radios. It is intended to be used as a supervisor and management board for the Ocelot compute/SDR board.

Features

  • Raspberry Pi RP2040 microcontroller
  • 2Mb Magnetoresistive RAM (MRAM)
  • HopeRF RFM98PW amplified LoRa module, with 1W transmit power (434MHz by default)
  • NiceRF LoRa1121 module (434MHz plus 2.4GHz by default)
  • Skytraq PX1122R or Orion B16 GNSS receiver
  • Filter and LNA frontend allows sharing UHF antenna between RFM98PW and SDR
  • Dual MCP2518FD CAN-FD interfaces
  • PC8523T I2C realtime clock
  • Multiple UARTs, intended for use across power domains
  • Power domain isolated "control interface" with UARTs and boot signals
  • Dual CSI camera interfaces
  • USB-C power and communications interface, for easy development and provisioning
  • USB-C (5V) or DC (5V to 28V) power inputs
  • "Remove before flight" jumpers to enter USB boot mode or disable hardware watchdog
  • Onboard system supervisor and watchdog
  • PC/104 mechanical form factor, suitable for industrial controls, cubesats, robotics, and more
  • Hirose DF13 connectors for additional interfaces
  • Compatible with a range of common cubesat PC/104 signal assignments
